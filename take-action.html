Take Action

Produce Less Waste. What Can You Do?

Start a transition towards a lifestyle that uses less products that creates waste. You can do this by boycotting products that do excessive harm to the environment and purchasing safer alternatives with different companies.

Action List

Check off each action once you feel you've completed it.

  • Step 1: Find out which household items cause unnecessary waste (too much plastic, poor design, excessive packaging, etc.). This does NOT mean every plastic item you own.
  • Step 2: Figure out the companies producing those items and stop buying from them.
  • Step 3: Do research about "too to good to be true" companies to avoid greenwashing.
  • Step 4: Buy from actual environmentally friendly companies (see "Resources").
  • Optional Step: Find a way to contact the manufacturers or give them feedback about products that are harmful.
